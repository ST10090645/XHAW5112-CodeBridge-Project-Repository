Garden Maintenance

Keep gardens healthy and beautiful using safe, water-wise practices. This six-week course focuses on essential maintenance for homes and small estates.

Six-week programme Fee: R750

Who this is for

  • Gardeners and grounds staff building professional skills.
  • Domestic workers who also handle outdoor areas.
  • Anyone tasked with lawn, beds, and simple irrigation.

What you’ll gain

  • Soil preparation, composting, and mulching basics.
  • Planting, seasonal care, and disease prevention.
  • Safe tool use and efficient watering schedules.

Course Modules

Soil & Compost Structure · Nutrients · Mulching ExpandCollapse
  • Soil types, pH basics, and improvement.
  • Compost methods and safe organic matter use.
  • Mulching to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
  • Fertiliser safety and application.
Planting & Care Seasonal timing · Spacing · Staking ExpandCollapse
  • Plant selection for climate and aspect.
  • Transplanting and bed preparation.
  • Weeding and basic pest identification.
  • Simple lawn care and edging.
Waterwise Irrigation Scheduling · Drip vs Sprinkler ExpandCollapse
  • Deep vs frequent watering and timing.
  • Simple drip lines and hose etiquette.
  • Greywater basics and restrictions.
  • Conserving water in dry months.
Pruning & Seasonal tasks Shrubs · Roses · Safety ExpandCollapse
  • Pruning goals and tool selection.
  • Deadheading, thinning, and shaping.
  • Winter/summer task lists.
  • Protective gear and safe disposal.
