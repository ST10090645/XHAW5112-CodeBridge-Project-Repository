First Aid

Become the calm, capable first responder at home and work. This six-month learnership covers essential first aid knowledge with lots of hands-on practice.

Six-month learnership Fee: R1500

Who this is for:

  • Domestic workers, caregivers, and community leaders.
  • Parents and guardians who want emergency readiness.
  • Anyone responsible for safety in the home or workplace.

What you’ll gain:

  • Confident scene assessment and emergency activation.
  • CPR, choking relief, and recovery position skills.
  • Wound, burn, and fracture management basics.

Course Modules

Scene Safety & Primary Survey DRSABCD · Calling for help ExpandCollapse
  • Danger checks, response, airway, breathing.
  • EMS activation and information to share.
  • Gloves and barrier protection.
  • Recovery position and monitoring.
CPR & Choking Adult · Child · Infant basics ExpandCollapse
  • Compression-only and full CPR basics.
  • Choking relief: back blows and thrusts.
  • Using an AED (overview).
  • Post-incident care and documentation.
Bleeding, Burns & Fractures Control · Cool · Support ExpandCollapse
  • Direct pressure and bandaging.
  • Burn cooling and covering.
  • Splinting and immobilisation basics.
  • When to escalate for professional help.
Medical Conditions Fainting · Seizures · Allergic reactions ExpandCollapse
  • Recognising common symptoms early.
  • Diabetes, asthma, and allergy basics.
  • Care until help arrives.
  • Hand-over to EMS and reporting.
