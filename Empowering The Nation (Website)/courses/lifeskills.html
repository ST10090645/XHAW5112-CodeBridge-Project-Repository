Life Skills

Build essential personal and workplace skills that open doors to stable employment. This six-month learnership focuses on communication, professionalism, financial basics, and job readiness.

Six-month learnership Fee: R1500

Who this is for:

  • People entering or returning to the workforce.
  • Domestic workers wanting stronger communication with employers.
  • Anyone seeking confidence with money, time, and planning.

What you’ll gain:

  • Clear communication, teamwork, and conflict basics.
  • Personal budgeting, saving, and responsible credit use.
  • CV writing, interview practice, and workplace rights.

Course Modules

Communication & Professionalism Listening · Clarity · Reliability ExpandCollapse
  • Professional behaviour and punctuality.
  • Giving/receiving feedback and resolving conflict.
  • Phone, messaging, and email etiquette.

  • Interview practice and employer questions.
  • Reference etiquette and follow-ups.

  • Personal hygiene and safe lifting.
  • Basic risk spotting and reporting.
  • Stress management and wellbeing.
