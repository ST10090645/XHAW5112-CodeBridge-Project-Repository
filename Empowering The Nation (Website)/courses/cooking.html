Cooking

Learn practical kitchen skills to confidently prepare nutritious, affordable meals. This six-week course blends safety, hygiene, and core techniques with South African household favourites.

Six-week programme Fee: R750

Who this is for:

  • Domestic workers responsible for meal prep and kitchen upkeep.
  • Beginners wanting reliable techniques on a budget.
  • Anyone needing healthy, family-friendly recipes and routines.

What you’ll gain:

  • Safe food handling, knife skills, and workstation setup.
  • Core methods: boiling, steaming, sautéing, baking, roasting.
  • Menu planning, shopping lists, and smart storage to reduce waste.

Course Modules

Kitchen safety & Hygiene Sanitation · Knife care · Cross-contamination ExpandCollapse
  • Handwashing, surface sanitation, and tool hygiene.
  • Knife skills: grip, dice, slice, and safe handling.
  • Preventing cross-contamination and safe thawing.
  • Gas/electric stove safety and first response basics.
Core cooking methods Moist & dry heat · Timing · Readyness ExpandCollapse
  • Boiling, steaming, sautéing, roasting, baking.
  • Seasoning and balancing flavour (salt, acid, heat, aroma).
  • Texture and doneness checks without gadgets.
  • Simple sauces, soups, and vegetable sides.
Nutrition & Planning Budgeting · Storage and Preservation · Weekly Menus ExpandCollapse
  • Affordable proteins, starches, and seasonal veg.
  • Batch cooking and cold/hot storage safety.
  • Shopping lists and pantry organisation.
  • Allergy awareness and substitutions.
South African favourites Everyday home meals ExpandCollapse
  • Pap & relish, simple stews, chakalaka basics.
  • One-pot meals and lunchbox ideas.
  • Vegetable-forward sides the whole family eats.
  • Time-savers for busy weekdays.
